A Sharp Colleague, Not Customer Support

The agent doesn't just report data — it diagnoses, recommends, and acts.

Diagnose
  • "GitHub's API gateway is throttling under load"
  • "Response times jumped from 200ms to 12s"
  • "47 events in retry queue, expire in 6 hours"
  • Reasoning trail shows investigation steps
Recommend
  • "Retry with 30s extended timeout"
  • "~80% should recover past the bottleneck"
  • "Retry again in 1h when load drops"
  • Specific and actionable, not generic
Act
  • Click "Retry" → agent reports progress live
  • Click "View Logs" → analysis → more actions
  • Click "Assign to AI Agent" → full autonomy
  • Proactive alerts when new incidents happen

Gaps Nobody Else Fills

Analyzed ChatGPT, Claude, Dust.tt, Intercom Fin, and Glean. Built what they're missing.

Data locked in prose
Metrics, tables, and sparklines are first-class elements — not paragraphs.
No inline trust signals
LIVE / FROM LOGS tags at the claim level. No other product ships this.
Answers stop at information
Action buttons trigger real responses. "Assign to AI Agent" delegates end-to-end.
Follow-up is broken
Contextual suggestion pills guide the next question. Every pill produces a rich response.
Chat can't show agent work
Reasoning trail shows investigation steps. Proactive alerts show live monitoring.
